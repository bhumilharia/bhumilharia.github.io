Who am I?

About

I'm currently working as an independent tech consultant. When not consulting or helping out friends with tech/processes, I'm busy learning how to make machines 'learn'.

Previous experience includes working at Pricebaba (Mumbai), the Microsoft HQ (Redmond), and internships at Pricebaba and Microsoft IDC (India Development Center). Worked (to varying extents) on large-scale web services infrastructure, automation infrastructure, telemetry, monitoring and alerting, deployment tech, crawling, etc. I find playing around with data fascinating, and have a keen interest in intelligent systems - and would love to work on those. Very interested in software design/architecture.

Other than tech - I like arts (admiring all the aesthetic, fancy stuff), meeting people, travel, adventure sports and trying out new stuff. And have a particularly strong interest in subjects related to social evolution - religion, war, history, spirituality, justice/morality and philosophy about work and happiness and life.

And I totally love Calvin and Hobbes.
